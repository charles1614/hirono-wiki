# B200

NVIDIA Blackwell datacenter GPU, second-generation tensor-core silicon at the high end.

## Observations

- JAX Scaling Book (Ch. 12, Dec 2025) provides B200 hardware spec: 148 SMs, 192GB HBM at 8 TB/s (vs H100's 80GB at 3.35 TB/s), 2.25 PFLOPs bf16, 4.5 PFLOPs fp8; adds TMEM (256kB/SM, Tensor Memory) because Blackwell TC accumulator no longer fits in SMEM/registers; TC size ~2048 FLOPs/cycle (vs H100's ~1024). B200 NVLink 5.0: 18 links × 50 GB/s = 900 GB/s per GPU. Critical intensity ≈ 281. — [[2025-12-11-how-to-think-about-gpus-how-to-scale-you]]
