# Memex

Vannevar Bush's 1945 thought experiment: a personal, curated knowledge store where the connections between documents ("associative trails") are as valuable as the documents themselves.

## Synthesis

Thin (1 source). Cited by [[Karpathy]] as the intellectual ancestor of LLM-maintained wikis. Bush's vision was closer to the LLM-wiki pattern than to what the web actually became — private, actively curated, with connections as first-class artifacts.

## Observations

- 1945 proto-wiki: personal curation + associative trails between documents. — [[2026-04-19-karpathy-llm-wiki-gist]]
- The thing Bush couldn't solve — "who does the maintenance" — is exactly what LLM-wikis now solve. — [[2026-04-19-karpathy-llm-wiki-gist]]
