# Walsh-Hadamard Transform

Fast linear transform used in TurboQuant and NVFP4 to decorrelate or bound outliers in activations before quantization, serving as the shared outlier-control primitive in both training-time (NVFP4) and inference-time (TurboQuant) compression.
