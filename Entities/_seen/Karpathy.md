# Karpathy

Andrej Karpathy. ML researcher (OpenAI, Tesla, Eureka Labs); prolific educator (nanoGPT, CS231n, the "Zero to Hero" lectures). Source of the LLM-wiki pattern this wiki implements.

## Synthesis

Thin (1 source). Referenced as the author of the LLM-wiki methodology; his phrasing ("Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase") crystallizes the division of labor. The wiki architecture in [[Meta/schema]] is a direct response to his three-layer design, adapted to Lark + Raindrop instead of Obsidian + local clipper.

## Observations

- Proposes a three-layer personal-wiki architecture (raw sources / LLM-owned wiki / governance schema) where the LLM does all bookkeeping and the human curates sources and asks questions. — [[2026-04-19-karpathy-llm-wiki-gist]]
- Distinguishes LLM-wikis from [[RAG]]: wikis compile knowledge once and keep it current; RAG re-derives on every query. — [[2026-04-19-karpathy-llm-wiki-gist]]
- Introduces the mental model: Obsidian = IDE, LLM = programmer, wiki = codebase. — [[2026-04-19-karpathy-llm-wiki-gist]]
