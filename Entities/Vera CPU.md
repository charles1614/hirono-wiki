- Launched at GTC 2026 as the world's first CPU purpose-built for agentic AI — 88 custom Olympus cores with NVIDIA Spatial Multithreading, LPDDR5X memory at 1.2 TB/s bandwidth (2× prior-gen at half the power), claims 2× energy efficiency and 50% faster performance vs traditional rack-scale CPUs. — [[2026-03-17-nvidia-launches-vera-cpu-purpose-built-f]]
- Integrates with [[Rubin]] GPUs via NVLink-C2C at 1.8 TB/s coherent bandwidth (7× PCIe Gen 6) within the Vera Rubin NVL72 platform; new Vera rack hosts 256 liquid-cooled CPUs for >22,500 concurrent CPU environments. — [[2026-03-17-nvidia-launches-vera-cpu-purpose-built-f]]
- Succeeds [[Grace CPU]]; in full production at GTC 2026 announcement, partner availability expected 2H 2026. Broad cloud adoption (Alibaba Cloud, ByteDance, CoreWeave, Meta, Oracle, et al.) and OEM support (Dell, HPE, Lenovo, Supermicro). — [[2026-03-17-nvidia-launches-vera-cpu-purpose-built-f]]
