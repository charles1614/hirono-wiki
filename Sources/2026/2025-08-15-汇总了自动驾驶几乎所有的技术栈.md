# [2025-08-01] 汇总了自动驾驶几乎所有的技术栈

## TL;DR

Promotional overview of the "自动驾驶之心" knowledge community, indexing ~40 autonomous driving technology tracks maintained within the community. Lists coverage areas including end-to-end AD, BEV perception, VLA, 3DGS/NeRF, world models, occupancy networks, trajectory prediction, SLAM, CUDA/deployment, and traditional planning-control.

## Key claims

- The community indexes 40+ technology tracks spanning perception (3D detection, BEV, multi-sensor fusion, occupancy, lane detection, segmentation, 2D detection, tracking), planning-control (search-based, sampling-based, MPC, trajectory prediction), simulation (Carla/Apollo/Autoware/3DGS closed-loop), and foundation models (VLA, VLM, diffusion, world models).
- Members drawn from top universities (PKU, Tsinghua, CMU, SJTU, NUS, ETH, NTU, HKUST) and industry (NIO, Li Auto, Xpeng, Horizon Robotics, Huawei, DJI, SAIC, Bosch, NVIDIA, Momenta, Baidu).
- Community curates 60+ autonomous driving datasets, major open-source projects per subdomain, job recruitment pipelines with direct referrals, and 100+ recorded technical talks.
- End-to-end AD track covers one-stage E2E, two-stage E2E, VLA algorithms (reasoning-enhanced, modular, and E2E VLA), and production mass-deployment discussions.
- RL track covers foundations + popular algorithms + VLM-RL integration; explicitly notes RL as a necessary component of VLM pipelines.
