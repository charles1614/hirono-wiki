# [2025-12-17] Mini3DV 2025 | 观点总结：世界模型前沿进展与技术展望

## TL;DR

Consensus report from the Mini3DV 2025 conference (60+ experts from academia and industry, Beijing, October 2025) systematizing world model progress from video generation toward physically grounded 4D simulation. Identifies four generational stages (G1–G4) and six technical axes: physical modeling, architecture unification, memory mechanisms, embodied AI, and 3D priors.

## Key claims

- World models are taxonomized into four generations: G1 (visual quality/Sora-era), G2 (consistent 3D/temporal), G3 (physically intrinsic faithfulness — current frontier), G4 (stochastic reasoning/long-horizon planning); the field is in transition from G1→G2/G3.
- "Augmented simulation" embeds explicit physics solvers into generative pipelines (RainyGS integrates shallow-water equations into 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) scenes, FieryGS integrates combustion equations) — preferred over pure data-driven generation which produces "physical hallucinations" near critical states.
- Architecture consensus converges on "AR backbone + per-token Diffusion Head": autoregressive for multi-modal reasoning/planning, diffusion decoder for high-fidelity visual output; LLaDA demonstrates discrete-text diffusion LLMs as viable.
- Sparse Diffusion Transformer (Sparse DiT) + WF-VAE (8×8×8 compression) reduces VRAM 2–5× and doubles encoding speed for 121-frame video generation.
- Memory mechanism split: explicit (3D Gaussian/Surfel as geometric memory, strong local consistency, expensive) vs. implicit (KV cache context, scales with model size, forgets early frames); RTFM "Posed Frames as Spatial Memory" proposes hybrid using weak 3D pose indexing without full reconstruction.
- World models as embodied pretraining base: [[VLA]] models pretrained on internet video (VPP from Tsinghua) decouple from robot-specific hardware data; experiments show steeper data-scaling curves — same capability with fewer real-robot samples.
- 4D world model datasets under construction: OmniWorld and 4DNeX each contain hundreds of millions of frames with depth, camera pose, and optical-flow annotations.
- Key open research problems: breaking AR/Diffusion duality, replacing data-driven scaling with physics-prior injection, and building hybrid memory that handles infinite-length sequences.
