# [2026-03-17] GTC2026 理想汽车詹锟 — MindVLA-o1 分享

## TL;DR

Slide-by-slide summary of Li Auto (理想汽车) AD engineer 詹锟's GTC 2026 presentation on MindVLA-o1, their next-generation Vision-Language-Action model for autonomous driving. Key advances include a novel encoder design, a generative world model with supervised training signal, MoE-based parallel action decoding, and RL with a world-model rollout mechanism.

## Key claims

- MindVLA-o1 is Li Auto's successor to their dual-system AD architecture; the talk explicitly addresses limitations of mainstream VLA paradigms before presenting the new design. — Source P2–P3
- The encoder features a novel design that is highlighted as a differentiator (details are in-slide, not described in text; see images). — Source P5
- MindVLA-o1 trains a **generative world model** and uses it as a supervised training signal for the action policy. — Source P6
- Action generation uses a **MoE parallel decoding mechanism** for efficient inference. — Source P7
- Reinforcement learning integrates **world-model rollouts** as part of the learning loop, enabling the policy to leverage future-state predictions during training. — Source P8
- Hardware-software co-design is cited as a key enabler for production efficiency. — Source P9
- The talk situates MindVLA-o1 within a broader Physical AI roadmap. — Source P10
