## TL;DR

A Chinese Docker image mirror proxy site (docker.aityp.com) that syncs 32,425+ container images from gcr.io, ghcr.io, quay.io, docker.io, registry.k8s.io and other registries, providing mainland-accessible mirrors totalling 39,624 GB synced and 4,838,009+ downloads.

## Key claims

- Supports 10 upstream registries: gcr.io (2,648 images), ghcr.io (3,183), quay.io (709), docker.io (24,290), registry.k8s.io (1,000), docker.elastic.co (293), skywalking.docker.scarf.sh (20), mcr.microsoft.com (127), k8s.gcr.io (140), docker.n8n.io (15).
- Single image size limit: 2 GB; on-demand sync with a queue (wait count shown live on site).
- Prominent ML/AI images included: `docker.io/vllm/vllm-openai`, `docker.io/pytorch/pytorch`, `docker.io/ollama/ollama`, `docker.io/gpustack/gpustack`, `docker.io/lmsysorg/sglang`, `ghcr.io/open-webui/open-webui`.
