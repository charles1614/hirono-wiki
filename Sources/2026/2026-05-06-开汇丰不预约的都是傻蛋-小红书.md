# 开汇丰不预约的都是傻蛋

## TL;DR

Tactical guide from a Hong Kong-based account-opening intermediary (港咔摆渡人) on the new-customer reward stack for opening [[HSBC]] cross-border accounts — Pulse / Premier / 卓越 — without going through the official online appointment system. Walking into the branch unannounced preserves "new customer" status and unlocks the full bonus.

## Key claims

- The online appointment workflow (官网提交预约) consumes the "new customer" identity (新客身份) — walk-in account opening is the only path that keeps the full reward eligibility.
- Four documented rewards as of the post's publication:
  - **IHG points**: 46,000 (~one night at a ~2K-RMB IHG property)
  - **Airline miles**: 26,000 points usable at Air China + China Southern
  - **HSBC cross-jurisdiction (mainland + Singapore)**: 320,000 HSBC points convertible to Cathay miles or HSBC mall items (Freshippo, JD gift cards)
  - **Remote-witness HK account opening**: 100-RMB subsidy + one free referral slot via old-customer-introduces-new
- Disclaimer: information aggregated from official public sources, not investment / financial advice.
