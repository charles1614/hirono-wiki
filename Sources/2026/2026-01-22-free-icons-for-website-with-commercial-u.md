# [2026-01-22] Free Icons for Website with Commercial Use — UXWing

## TL;DR

UXWing offers a free collection of SVG and PNG icons licensed for commercial and personal use without attribution. Icons are compressed ~60% without quality loss and organized across 150+ categories.

## Key claims

- All icons are free for commercial use with no attribution required; the license explicitly permits personal and commercial projects.
- Icons are available in both SVG (vector, retina-friendly, smaller file size) and PNG (transparent background) formats; SVG is recommended for large projects.
- The library includes 150+ categories and is actively updated; users can submit requests for new icons.
