# [2025-11-03] 理想ICCV'25分享了世界模型：从数据闭环到训练闭环

## TL;DR

Li Auto VLA lead 詹锟 presented at ICCV'25 on evolving autonomous driving from data closed-loop to training closed-loop, centering on a VLA+RL+World Model stack for L4 training. The key thesis: data closed-loop alone cannot solve long-tail scenarios; a training closed-loop using world models, RL, and synthetic data is the next phase.

## Key claims

- Li Auto's end-to-end MPI (Miles Per Intervention) has improved ~19x since July 2024, now exceeding 220+.
- The training closed-loop tech stack comprises three pillars: **scene reconstruction** (Feedforward 3DGS, multi-pass, style transfer), **synthetic data generation** (video/point-cloud generation, neural rendering), and **reinforcement learning** (SimAgents, 3D assets, reward models).
- L4 training loop core: VLA Diffusion + world-model-based RL; RL covers RLHF, RLVR, and RLAIF.
- 1.5 billion km of driving data collected; 200+ triggers used to extract 15–45s clip data with ~1 min upload latency.
- Data closed-loop cannot handle long-tail events (traffic control, fireworks, sudden lane changes); simulation-based training loop is required.
- Interactive SimAgents are identified as a key challenge for closing the training loop.
