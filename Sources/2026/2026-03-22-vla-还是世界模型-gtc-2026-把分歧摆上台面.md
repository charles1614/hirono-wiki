# [2026-03-22] VLA 还是世界模型？GTC 2026 把分歧摆上台面

## TL;DR

GTC 2026 surfaced a live architectural debate: [[VLA]]-first approaches (NVIDIA, 理想汽车) versus world-model-first approaches (宇树科技). By the end of the conference the two camps were converging — VLA models are adding predictive latent world models, while world-model advocates are facing real-time and engineering constraints that push them toward VLA-style action heads.

## Key claims

- [[NVIDIA Alpamayo]] 1.5 now supports natural-language instruction following, navigation-informed path decisions, and verbal commands (turn left/right, stop, avoid); NVIDIA targets consumer L4 delivery in 2028, with L4 scale testing in 2027.
- 至简动力 (CEO: 贾鹏, formerly Li Auto) proposed **LaST₀** — a Mixture-of-Transformer base model unifying 2D/3D/proprioception prediction, fast-slow system, and Policy-Critic in one architecture; achieved SOTA on simulated and real tasks and was accepted to CVPR 2026.
- 理想汽车 released **MindVLA-o1**, a next-generation [[VLA]] architecture with native multimodal MoE Transformer, a 3D ViT encoder trained via self-supervised next-frame prediction (learning geometry + semantics + motion jointly), a predictive latent world model, and RL fine-tuning infrastructure (MindSim + RL Infra).
- 宇树科技 CEO 王兴兴 stated world models and video generation have "no visible ceiling" and a higher success probability than [[VLA]] models, which he argued have lower ceilings and unresolved generalization problems.
- 宇树's RL locomotion control stack reached V5 (full-body dynamic teleoperation, any motion); their G1 robot used a pre-trained whole-body RL model (not task-specific) at the 2026 Spring Festival Gala, with a 128-line 3D LiDAR and a cluster control system managing dozens of robots simultaneously.
- 银河通用 (CEO: 王鹤) declared 2026 the "application year" for embodied intelligence; their robots are deployed in 20 cities with >2,000 daily users; they received 1,000+ unit orders in 2025 and plan thousands of units of autonomous deployment in 2026.
