# [2026-01-27] 腾讯混元AI Infra核心技术重磅开源：推理吞吐提升30%！

## TL;DR

Tencent Hunyuan AI Infra team open-sources [[HPC-Ops]], a production-grade high-performance LLM inference operator library built with CUDA and CuTe from scratch, targeting Chinese deployment-grade inference cards (H20 etc.) rather than H800. Key results: 30% QPM improvement on Hunyuan models, 17% on [[DeepSeek]], with Attention up to 2.22× over FlashInfer/FlashAttention, GroupGEMM up to 1.88× over [[DeepGEMM]], and FusedMoE up to 1.49× over [[TensorRT-LLM]].

## Key claims

- [[HPC-Ops]] achieves end-to-end QPM improvements of 30% on Hunyuan models and 17% on [[DeepSeek]] models in production over existing operator libraries.
- Attention kernel on [[H20]]-class inference cards: BF16 decode improves 1.35×–2.22× over SOTA; FP8 decode improves 1.09×–2.0× at large sequence lengths; BF16 prefill (128–64K tokens) improves up to 1.3× at small batch, aligns with SOTA at large batch.
- GroupGEMM at batch ≤ 64 (low-latency scenario): up to 1.88× over [[DeepGEMM]] v2.2.0; ~1.1× advantage maintained at large batch. Key technique: AB matrix swap for Decode Attention and small-batch GroupGEMM to align with `wgmma` hardware instruction, achieving >80% of memory bandwidth peak.
- FusedMoE (complete pipeline: data reorder + GroupGEMM + Reduce-weighted-average): up to 1.49× over [[TensorRT-LLM]] v1.1.0 in TP mode; up to 1.09× in EP-simulated balanced scenario. Adaptive reordering strategy for different input lengths.
- Key engineering innovations: (1) instruction-level AB swap alignment for `wgmma` to hit >80% memory bandwidth peak on inference cards; (2) Interleave reordering for FP8 Attention kernel eliminating thread-level data shuffle; (3) CuTe-based `vec` abstraction layer unifying efficient data movement; (4) persistent kernel pattern hiding prologue/epilogue overhead; (5) data reordering reducing temporary VRAM usage.
- API compatible with [[vLLM]] and [[SGLang]] inference frameworks; natively supports BF16 and FP8 quantization. GitHub: [github.com/Tencent/hpc-ops](https://github.com/Tencent/hpc-ops).
