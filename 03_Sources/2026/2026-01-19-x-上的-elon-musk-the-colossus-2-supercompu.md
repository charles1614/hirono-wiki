# [2026-01-17] Colossus 2 Supercomputer Now Operational — World's First Gigawatt Training Cluster

## TL;DR

Elon Musk announced that Colossus 2, the xAI supercomputer powering Grok, went operational on 2026-01-17 as the world's first 1 GW AI training cluster, with an upgrade to 1.5 GW planned for April 2026.

## Key claims

- Colossus 2 is described as the "first Gigawatt training cluster in the world," operational as of January 17, 2026.
- The cluster is dedicated to training [[Grok]] and is slated to scale to 1.5 GW in April 2026.
- Located in Memphis, TN; operated by [[xAI]].
