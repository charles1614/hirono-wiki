# [2025-11-11] 在地平线搞自动驾驶的这三年

## TL;DR

Research engineer candywisdom reflects on three years of autonomous driving R&D at Horizon Robotics before transitioning to embodied intelligence, summarizing seven interconnected projects spanning multi-camera 3D perception ([[Sparse4D]]), end-to-end planning ([[SparseDrive]]), trajectory prediction, traffic simulation, sensor simulation, and a unified foundation model (LATR).

## Key claims

- [[Sparse4D]] v1 introduced sparse-query deformable aggregation for multi-view 3D perception; v2 reduced temporal complexity from O(T) to O(1) via recurrent instance features; v3 added joint detection + tracking with zero tracking labels, holding first place on nuScenes camera-only detection and tracking leaderboards since October 2023.
- [[SparseDrive]] extends Sparse4D to five tasks (detection, tracking, mapping, prediction, planning) and achieves end-to-end open-loop evaluation on nuScenes, though closed-loop performance remains a work-in-progress due to simplistic planning decoder.
- EDA (Evolving and Distinct Anchors) addresses the under-studied anchor assignment problem in trajectory prediction, using dynamic anchors + NMS matching to model multi-modal distributions with plug-and-play compatibility.
- UniMM unifies continuous mixture and GPT-like discrete traffic-flow simulation models into a single framework, enabling closed-loop multi-agent rollout.
- DriveCamSim uses explicit 3D projection constraints to generate sensor images at arbitrary frame rate, camera intrinsics, and extrinsics — decoupling temporal and spatial generation entirely; supports 3D bboxes, maps, ego pose, and reference images as conditions.
- LATR (Look Around Transformer) performs masked-image-modeling pretraining across seven unified tasks (3D det/track, mapping, pose estimation, prediction, planning, depth, NVS) with only a new query-to-task MLP per task; never published as a paper.
- Author argues evaluation infrastructure is the bottleneck: beyond ~50 MPI, real-vehicle testing is impractical, and RL-based policy improvement is only unlocked once simulation evaluation matures.
