# [2025-08-06] 字节跳动 VeOmni 框架开源：统一多模态训练效率飞跃

## TL;DR

[[ByteDance]] open-sourced [[VeOmni]], a unified multi-modal training framework built jointly by the Seed team, Volcano Engine ML Platform, and IaaS Heterogeneous Computing team. It supports LLMs, VLMs, and DiT-based video generation models (480P/720P T2V/I2V) under a single API with [[FSDP]], Ulysses, and Expert Parallel strategies, achieving 40%+ throughput gains over comparable open-source solutions on [[Wan 2.1]] models.

## Key claims

- VeOmni unifies three axes: multi-modal workloads, parallelism strategies, and compute substrate — supporting LoRA, [[FSDP]], Ulysses, and Expert Parallel as composable primitives selected via a launch script.
- Selective recomputation: ranks forward tensors by ROI (memory saved per microsecond of extra compute); `gate1_mul` saves ~40 MB for 180 µs vs `down_proj`'s 4000 µs for the same savings — a 22× difference exploited to cut recompute ratio from 60% to 30%.
- On [[Wan 2.1]]-14B LoRA: I2V 720P is 48%+ faster on compute-type cards and 59.5%+ faster on memory-bandwidth-type cards vs open-source baselines; T2V 720P 44.4%/57.4% respectively; Wan2.1-1.3B T2V 480P 51%+ faster.
- FSDP + Ulysses combination reduces peak memory to 45% of baseline for 480P/720P T2V/I2V tasks.
- Kernel-level fusion: rewrites Attention–FFN–residual as a single kernel for DiT small-op chains, reducing memory-access count by hundreds of times.
- Supports trajectory distillation, DMD, and auto-regressive distillation natively; DMD can distill to as few as 4-step inference while maintaining generation quality.
- Used internally to train UI-Tars 1.5 and verified at thousand-GPU scale; integrated into Volcano Engine ML Platform and TrainingKit.
- Checkpoint layout: `global_step_xxx/{extra_state,hf_ckpt,model,optimizer}/` with HuggingFace-compatible safetensors under `hf_ckpt/`.
