# [2026-03-18] 阶跃星辰开源全量 SFT 数据集

## TL;DR

StepFun (阶跃星辰) announces full open-source release of the Step 3.5 Flash SFT dataset: 17B tokens of high-quality multi-turn dialogues and chain-of-thought reasoning data, along with tokenizer, preprocessed training shards, and the SteptronOss training framework. The release reached #1 on HuggingFace Dataset Trending within 24 hours.

## Key claims

- Step 3.5 Flash SFT dataset contains **17B tokens** of multi-turn dialogue and CoT reasoning; released on HuggingFace under StepFun's page. — Source body
- The full open-source package includes: pretrained weights, mid-training weights, the **SteptronOss** training framework, and the SFT dataset — all four components released simultaneously. — Source body
- Reached **#1 on HuggingFace Dataset Trending** within 24 hours of release and received positive reception on Reddit r/LocalLLaMA. — Source body
- StepFun provides preprocessed training shards and tokenizer alongside raw data, lowering the barrier for direct model fine-tuning. — Source body
