# [2025-12-14] 一文读懂英伟达最新开源VLA大模型

## TL;DR

Technical deep-dive into [[NVIDIA Alpamayo]]-R1 (Alpamayo-R1), NVIDIA's open-sourced reasoning-enhanced VLA model for autonomous driving. Introduces Chain of Causation (CoC) structured reasoning, a modular multi-camera VLA architecture with up to 20× token compression, and a three-stage training pipeline (action modality injection → SFT → GRPO RL alignment).

## Key claims

- Alpamayo-R1 addresses a core deficiency in free-form reasoning VLAs: lack of explicit causal grounding and reasoning-action consistency; CoC framework enforces three principles — decision grounding (single explicit decision per trace), causal locality (evidence only from observed history window), annotation economy (only decision-relevant factors).
- CoC dataset taxonomy covers 16 closed-set driving decisions (longitudinal: speed tracking, following, adapting, gap-seeking, overtaking, yielding; lateral: lane-keeping, merge, micro-shift, lane-change, curb approach, turning) and open-set critical components (objects, traffic lights, yield/stop controls, road events, lane markings, path intent, ODD constraints).
- VLM backbone is Cosmos-Reason, trained on 3.7M VQA samples + 24,700 driving-scene VQA samples distilled from [[DeepSeek-R1]], plus 100K physical-AI supplementary samples spanning autonomous driving, robotics, healthcare, smart city, manufacturing.
- Three visual encoding strategies: single-image tokenizer (default), Triplane multi-camera tokenizer (fixed token count independent of camera count, ~4× compression), Flex video tokenizer (~20× compression while maintaining or improving driving performance).
- Action decoder uses unicycle-dynamics representation (acceleration + curvature) rather than raw (x, y) coordinates; flow-matching action expert decodes to 64 waypoints at 10 Hz over 6 seconds — faster than autoregressive sampling and kinematically feasible.
- Three-part GRPO reward: reasoning quality (scored by large reasoning model like [[DeepSeek-R1]]), reasoning-action consistency (rule-based match of described meta-action vs predicted trajectory), trajectory quality (L2 distance to expert trajectory + collision penalty + jerk penalty).
- Benchmark results vs. baselines: open-loop planning minADE6@6s improved 12%; closed-loop off-road rate reduced 35% (17%→11%); close-encounter rate reduced 25% (4%→3%); RL post-training raised reasoning quality LRM score 45% (3.1→4.5), reasoning-action consistency 37% (0.62→0.85); end-to-end inference latency on NVIDIA RTX 6000 Pro: 99 ms.
