# [2025-05-27] 从1到∞: 多模态大模型知识面试一本通

## TL;DR

A living Feishu wiki (40+ chapters) by "代码熊" covering multimodal LLM interview prep, written by a Tsinghua CS grad with 15+ SSP-tier offers. Covers VLM architectures (Qwen-VL, DeepSeek-VL, InternVL, SigLIP), OCR models, multimodal RL (DAPO/VeRL), RAG, and model-by-model update tracking through mid-2026.

- Three-part structure: fundamentals (ViT, projector design), fast-track interview handbook (true exam questions), and rolling model-by-model breakdown.
- Covers PatchMerger evolution in [[Qwen]]-VL series, dynamic resolution via NaViT, SigLIP2 encoder, and MLP vs Q-Former projector comparisons.
- multimodal RL section covers DAPO algorithm + [[verl]] framework for training VLMs with reasoning ("Think with images").
- OCR VLM landscape (DeepSeek OCR, HunyuanOCR with GRPO, dots.ocr at 1.7B) with inter-model comparisons.
- Multimodal embedding models: VLM2Vec, BGE-VL, GME (Qwen2-VL-based), and VRAG-RL training framework.
- Written by a 3-person team (代码熊 + Tsinghua MSc + PhD), updated weekly; multimodal entries also cross-posted to the LLM interview handbook.
