**Pain points surfaced** (captured for schema/tool follow-ups, not fixed in this commit):

1. **PDF-as-image arxiv slugs are unusable.** Half the arxiv ingest-ready pool fetched as page-PNG renderings with zero extractable text — only the `/abs/<id>` URLs got abstract text, the `/pdf/<id>` ones got image-only `content.md`. Originally planned to ingest 5 such; substituted with 4 text-rich slugs. Fixes: (a) prefer `/abs/` URLs in raindrop captures, (b) vision-mode reading pass on PDF-as-image slugs, (c) explicit arxiv text-extraction fallback.
2. **Slug truncation at 60 chars cuts mid-word.** Schema mandates `<slug> ≤ 60 chars`; pretraining-large-language-models-with-n is the canonical ugly result. The raw-archive contract forces Source slug = raw slug, propagating the truncation everywhere. Options: relax to 80, truncate at word boundary, or decouple Source slug from raw slug.
3. **`tools/bin/reindex.ts` REPO_ROOT bug fixed in commit `888fd53`** — was resolving to `tools/` not the repo root; broke on the empty-state run after fresh-start.
4. **No `regen Synthesis` tool** — entities created in this batch have placeholder Synthesis blocks ("regenerated from Observations below"); nothing actually regenerates them. Karpathy-gap #3 from `Meta/references/karpathy-llm-wiki-gist.md` analysis.

Time: ~90 min wall clock for 10 sources (Sources only — entity/topic stubs created in batch). At sustainable pace, Day 1 (5–10 sources hand-ingested) is genuinely feasible. The compounding signal is real — 3 promotions on first batch.
